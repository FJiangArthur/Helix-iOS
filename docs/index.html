Features

Everything you need, right on your lenses

A full-featured companion app that brings AI intelligence directly to your field of view.

🎤

Real-Time Transcription

Live speech-to-text with question detection and conversation intelligence powered by on-device Apple Speech Recognition.

🤖

Multi-AI Support

Choose from OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek, Qwen, Zhipu, and SiliconFlow. Bring your own API key and pick the model you prefer.

💻

Smart HUD Display

Rich bitmap dashboard with clock, weather, stocks, calendar, and notifications rendered directly on your glasses.

💬

Conversation Modes

General, Interview (with STAR coaching), and Passive listening modes tailored to different situations.

⚡

Delta Updates

Efficient bitmap rendering with incremental BLE transfers for instant HUD refresh with minimal battery impact.

🔒

Privacy First

Local processing, encrypted API keys, no tracking, and no analytics. Your data stays on your device.

How It Works

Three simple steps

Get up and running in minutes with your Even Realities G1 glasses.

1

Connect

Pair your Even Realities G1 glasses via Bluetooth. The app discovers and connects automatically.

2

Listen

Start a conversation and let the AI analyze speech in real time with on-device transcription.

3

See

AI insights, facts, and suggestions appear on your glasses HUD as the conversation unfolds.
