Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Even Companion ("the App") is developed by Art Jiang. This privacy policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information. We believe in transparency and minimal data collection — the App is designed to keep your data on your device.

2. Data We Collect

  • Audio: Microphone audio is captured temporarily for real-time transcription. Audio is processed on-device via Apple Speech Recognition and is not stored permanently or sent to our servers.
  • Calendar & Reminders: Read-only access to display upcoming events on your glasses HUD. Data stays on device and is never transmitted.
  • Location: Approximate location is used solely to fetch local weather for the glasses HUD. Your location is not stored or shared.
  • API Keys: Your LLM provider API keys (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) are stored in iOS Keychain (encrypted). They are sent only to the provider you select — never to us.
  • Conversation History: Stored locally on your device only. Not uploaded anywhere.

3. Third-Party Services

The App sends conversation text to the LLM provider you choose, using your API key. We do not operate any servers or intermediary services.

Supported providers: OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek, Qwen, Zhipu AI, and SiliconFlow. Each provider has their own privacy policy governing how they handle data sent to their APIs.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties. Period.

5. Tracking

The App does not track you. There are no analytics SDKs, no advertising frameworks, and no App Tracking Transparency prompt — because there is nothing to track.

6. Data Storage & Security

  • API keys are encrypted via iOS Keychain, Apple's hardware-backed secure storage.
  • Conversation history is stored in the local app sandbox, protected by iOS data protection.
  • There is no cloud sync, no remote database, and no server-side storage of any user data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to check this page periodically for the latest information.
