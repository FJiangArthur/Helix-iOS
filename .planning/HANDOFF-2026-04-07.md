The three hardware-found fixes in `03c1bdc`:
1. **Glasses HUD flashing during streaming.** `DashboardService._refreshDashboardIfActive` was pushing a "REC HH:MM\nMon Apr ..." conversation-stats text frame on the same `0x4e/0x71` channel the LLM streams to via `0x4e/0x30/0x31`. Two writers to one channel; firmware tore down the AI screen on every dashboard frame. Fix: suppress the conversation-stats refresh when `EngineStatus` is `thinking` or `responding`.
2. **Live transcription dropping ~3-5s of speech on every Q&A press.** `_runManualContextualQa` was calling `pauseTranscription()` for the entire LLM call duration. Fix: removed the pause/resume entirely. Voice-feedback prevention belongs in the voice assistant pipeline, not in killing all transcription.
3. **Cannot scroll up while a streaming answer is rendering.** `HomeScreen._scrollToBottom` was unconditional. Fix: skip auto-scroll when user is more than 64px from the bottom.
